Our Story

Empowering the next generation to connect with their roots through language.

Our Mission

Yuvagana is the youth wing of **Samskrita Bharati USA (SBUSA)**. Our mission is to build a vibrant network of college students and young professionals who are passionate about learning and promoting Samskritam.

We believe that Samskritam is not just an ancient language, but a bridge to a rich cultural heritage, philosophy, and a global community of thinkers.

YUVAGANA + SBUSA

Who is our Audience?

We cater to young adults under 30—including college students looking for campus connections (CSN) and early-career professionals seeking a cultural balance (SNYP).

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining SB Yuva.

What is the age group for SB Yuva? +

SB Yuva is primarily for college students (College Samskritam Network) and young professionals (SNYP). Generally, this covers individuals between the ages of 18 and 35.

Do I need any prior knowledge of Samskritam? +

Absolutely not! We have tracks for absolute beginners (L1/Pravesha) as well as advanced learners. We welcome everyone regardless of their starting point.

How can I start a chapter at my university? +

It's easy! Reach out to us via the "Join Us" form or email info@sbyuva.org. Our CSN coordinators will provide you with a toolkit and guide you through the process of getting recognized by your student union.

Is SB Yuva connected to Samskrita Bharati USA? +

Yes, SB Yuva is the youth wing of Samskrita Bharati USA. We follow the same pedagogical methods but tailor our content and activities for the young adult demographic.
